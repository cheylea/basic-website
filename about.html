About


Who is Suki?

Suki is a small ginger cat that lives in Bournemouth, England with owners Cheylea and Edward.


Facts about Suki


  • Suki is a female cat, making her much rarer than male cats of similar colouring. (Read more about colouring rarity here).
  • She weighs under 2 kg, making her unusually small and very easy to carry around. (See more cute small cats here!).
  • She has one half brother JoJo who is always trying to get her into mischief.
  • Nicknames for Suki include: 'Bubba Girl', 'Princess Baby Angel' and 'Little Lady'.
